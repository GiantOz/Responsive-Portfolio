About Me

My name is Bryant Osborne and I am a new Web Developer. I attended school in Philadelphia Pa. I became interested in computer coding years ago when I attended a computer class with my father. Since then I have remained interested but never pursued a career in the field, until now! Now, I am doing something I truly enjoy! I had no idea that this would be as interesting and fun as this is, I only wish I had done this sooner.

After I graduated High School I joined the US Army and recieved an Honorable Discharge. I then attended various colleges and worked for the City of Philadelphia. In 2004 I decided to quit my city job and work for myself, I now own Top Flight Facility Maintenance and we have multiple clients and have a lucrative business. It was not until I saw an advertisement for University of Penn that I decided that I could realize my earlier dream of computer coding and it was the best decision I have ever made.
